Most of the top 100 movies in major genres have titles that are short and sweet - generally below 20 characters.

Step 3: Release at the right time

While movies released during all parts of the year can fall in the top ranks, certain periods of the year have seen more hits. An appropriately-timed release can further help make drive the success of your film's sales. For example, more horror films that have made the annual Top 100 were released in October (likely when people are in the haunted Halloween spirit.)
